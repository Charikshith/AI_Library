What you'll learn

Whether you're new to system design or preparing for tough interviews, this course gives you the structured thinking to design robust, scalable systems — for real-world impact, not just whiteboarding.

  • Reason about latency, throughput, and trade-offs intuitively
  • Apply the 4-step design framework to any problem
  • Choose between monolith, microservices, or event-driven
  • Design real systems: YouTube, Uber, Google Docs, and more
  • Recognize anti-patterns before they cost you
